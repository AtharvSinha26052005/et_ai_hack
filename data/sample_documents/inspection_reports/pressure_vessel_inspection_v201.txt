INTERNAL INSPECTION FINDINGS
==============================
Finding 1 (MAJOR):
   Location: Bottom head, 6 o'clock position near drain nozzle
   Description: Cladding disbondment observed over approximately 200mm x 150mm area.
   SS321 cladding lifted from base metal. Underlying SA516 shows surface corrosion.
   UT spot check on base metal shows 37.0mm (within limits).
   Recommendation: Weld overlay repair per approved WPS during next turnaround.
   Regulation Reference: API-510, ASME Section VIII

Finding 2 (MINOR):
   Location: Shell Course 2, 180° position
   Description: Higher corrosion rate (0.25 mm/yr) compared to other locations.
   Possible localized flow impingement from inlet nozzle N1. Cladding intact.
   Recommendation: Add CML-07 to enhanced monitoring — re-measure in 12 months.
   No immediate action required.

Finding 3 (MINOR):
   Location: Manhole gasket seating surface
   Description: Light scoring marks on raised face. Depth < 0.5mm.
   Recommendation: Use spiral wound gasket with inner ring (Type R) at reassembly.
   Surface acceptable per ASME PCC-1 guidelines.

Finding 4 (OBSERVATION):
   Location: Internal baffles
   Description: Slight bowing of baffle plates observed (max 5mm deflection).
   Does not affect vessel integrity. Flow distribution may be marginally affected.
   Recommendation: Monitor during next inspection. No action required.

EXTERNAL INSPECTION FINDINGS
==============================
Finding 5 (MINOR):
   Location: Support saddle region, underside of vessel
   Description: Paint coating deterioration at saddle-to-shell contact area.
   No corrosion of base metal detected. UT spot check: 37.8mm (satisfactory).
   Recommendation: Repaint during next available opportunity (minimum SSPC-SP6
   surface preparation + 2-coat epoxy system, 250 microns DFT).

Finding 6 (OBSERVATION):
   Location: External insulation, upper section
   Description: Two areas of damaged aluminum jacketing identified (total ~0.5m²).
   No moisture ingress detected (confirmed by UT from external surface).
   Recommendation: Replace jacketing and seal with silicone sealant to prevent CUI.
